RPL at UCSB

Our mission is to provide ambitious aerospace opportunities for students eager to pursue similar work throughout their careers. The rigorous engineering and safety challenges associated with building flying machines will give students the skills they need to thrive in any field of engineering they choose. Currently, our primary goal is to compete in the Far Mars Rocket competition.
